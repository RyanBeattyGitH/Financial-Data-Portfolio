# Analysis Results for EPL 2018/19

## Summary of Findings

### Total Matches Played
380 matches were played in the 2018/19 season.

### Home Wins
Home teams won a total of 181 matches, indicating a strong home advantage during this season.

### Average Goals per Match
The average number of goals scored per match was approximately 2.82, up from the previous season which had 2.68.

## Conclusions
The 2018/19 season showcased significant home team advantages, with almost 48% of matches being won by the home side.
The scoring average indicates an exciting season, with over a 5% increase of goals from the previous season.
